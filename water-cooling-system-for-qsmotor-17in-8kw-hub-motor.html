Water Cooling System for QSMotor 17in 8kW Hub Motor

So I converted a Kawasaki Z650 to an electric drivetrain using this hub motor for the rear wheel.  The motorcycle does ~55-60mph with a power consumption of roughly ~6-8kW with roughly ~120A of phase current.  Over extended periods of time at this speed, the motor will tend to overheat, reaching internal temperatures of 90C after 20 minutes, and reaching 130C for short bursts at ~600A of phase current.  Therefore, a water cooling system was developed to keep temperatures under control and boost power output.
